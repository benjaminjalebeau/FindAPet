Home

Almost everyone at some point in their life have this thought... "I want a puppy." While this idea is exciting, adopting a dog that doesn't fit your lifestyle can make you and your new pet's quality of life poorer. This website will help you find a dog breed that matches well with your lifestyle, or it might suggest that a dog isn't the right fit for at this point in your life.

Fill out both forms below, click save for both, and then select the breed tab in the navigation bar. These selections will be used to find dog breeds that match with your answers. Pay attention to the message on top of the matching breeds page, for it will let you know how well the returned breeds will fit your lifestyle. Note that the application will only return the best matches.
